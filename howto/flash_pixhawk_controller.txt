This software is compatible with version 3.4.6 of the px4fmu firmware.

you can find this version in the folder firmware.

To flash the firmware:


- Connect the flight controller to the PC through a USB cable
- open a terminal and type:
$ roscd uri_uav/../firmware
$ python px_uploader.py --port /dev/ttyACM0 ArduCopter-v2.px4

- After starting the script, press the reset button (a small hole in the center of one side of the flight controller) on your PX4FMU to make it enter bootloader mode. 

- wait for the board to reset and finish all the tunes

- good to go
